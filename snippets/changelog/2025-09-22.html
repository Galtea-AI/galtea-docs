Organizations can now enhance their security by configuring SAML SSO for authentication, allowing for seamless and secure access to the Galtea platform through your existing identity provider.

Improved Misuse Resilience Metric

The Misuse Resilience metric has been enhanced to accept the full product context, allowing for a more accurate and comprehensive evaluation of your model's ability to resist misuse.

New Analytics Filter

The analytics page now includes a filter for Test Case language. This allows you to narrow down your analysis and gain more precise insights into the performance of your multilingual models.
