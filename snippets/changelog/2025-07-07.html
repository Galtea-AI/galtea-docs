Custom Threats, New Metrics, and Enhanced Test Case Management

2025-06-09

Tailor Your Red Teaming with Custom Threats

You can now define your own custom threats when creating Red Teaming Tests. This new capability allows you to create highly specific adversarial tests that target the unique vulnerabilities of your AI product.


New Red Teaming Strategies: RolePlay and Prefix Injection

We've expanded our arsenal of Red Teaming Strategies to help you build more robust AI defenses:

  • RolePlay: Attempts to alter the model's identity, encouraging it to ignore its safety mechanisms to perform actions it would otherwise refuse.
  • Prefix Injection: Adds a misleading or tactical instruction before the actual threat to influence model behavior.

Introducing the Misuse Resilience Metric

A new Red Teaming metric, Misuse Resilience, is now available. It evaluates if your product's responses stay aligned with its product description, even when facing adversarial inputs or out-of-scope requests. This helps ensure your AI stays on-task and doesn't get diverted into performing unintended actions.


Enhanced Test Case Management: Mark as Reviewed

To improve collaboration for human annotation teams, Test Cases can now be marked as "reviewed." This helps you track which test cases have been validated and see who performed the review, providing a clear audit trail.
