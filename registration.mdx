## Request Access to Galtea

Currently, registration to our platform is available upon request. Follow the steps below to get access to Galtea.

### Registration Process

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="envelope" title="Send us an email">
    You can request access by sending an email to
    [info@galtea.ai](mailto:info@galtea.ai?subject=Registration%20Request&body=Hello,%0D%0A%0D%0AI%20would%20like%20to%20request%20access%20to%20Galtea.%0D%0A%0D%0AMy%20details:%0D%0A%20-%20Name:%20%0D%0A%20-%20Company:%20%0D%0A%20-%20Position:%20%0D%0A%20-%20Purpose%20of%20use:%20%0D%0A%0D%0AThank%20you.)
    with the following information: 
    - Name
    - Company
    - Position
    - Purpose of use
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="globe" title="Contact through website">
    You can also visit our [contact page](https://galtea.ai/#contact) on the Galtea website to fill out the contact form and request access to the platform.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Note>After submitting your request, our team will review your application and get back to you as soon as possible with next steps to complete your registration.</Note>
